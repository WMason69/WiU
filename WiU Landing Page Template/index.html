Project Name: WorldSurf

Project Description

Welcome to the Project WorldSurf website. You have landed at the premiere destination for surfing enthusiasts and experts alike. We have taken the liberty of gathering both user and scientifically generated information about surfing conditions throughout the world. As you browse our site we are certain that you will find our information to be invaluable as you prepare for your next surfing excursion. Additionally, when you go on your next adventure, feel free to record and submit any data that you feel would be useful in helping our site to be even more accurate and beneficial to fellow users.
